CSN ReDesign

Timeline: 1 week
Tasks:
  • Identify design patterns
  • Communicate an annotated GUI
  • Find an opportunity for redesign
  • Mock up and user testing
Tools: Adobe XD

What is the app used for?

CSN app is issued by the Swedish National Board of Student Aid for students who are receiving grants/loans
  • Check if a payment back from the student is received, and the remaining unpaid amount.
  • Check the date when a student gets paid.
  • Hand in study insurances.
  • Other services that include practice information about students loans/grants.

What are the issues?

After studying the app and asking 15 users about the issues they have with it, the following problems stood out:
  • Users find the layout and overall visual design of the app outdated and uninviting to use.
  • The users have to navigate back to the home screen few times before viewing all the necessary information they are after.
  • All of the options on the home screen contained in one-sized boxes, even though 2-3 of them are used a lot more than the others.
  • All users preferred using the website because everything they needed was displayed on a single page.
